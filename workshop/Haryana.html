About Haryana.

Haryana is a jewel in the crown of the country India, sharing its capital Chandigarh with the state of Punjab. The Shivalik hills borders the state in the north-east and the Aravalli range towards the south and the commonly spoken language of the natives in this state is Haryanvi. Haryana has a number of sites which dates back to the ancient civilizations of the Vedic era. The state also has the history of being the battleground for the Indian epic Mahabharata and also for the well known Panipat battle.
