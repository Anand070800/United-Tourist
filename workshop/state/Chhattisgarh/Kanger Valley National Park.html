About Kanger Valley Park.

Chhattisgarh is known for its natural beauty and has beautiful caves all over the region, some of them which are undiscovered as well. The Kailash Kutumb Caves inside Kanger Valley National Park is one of the biggest attractions. These caves have several limestone layers formed over the years which are amazing to see.
