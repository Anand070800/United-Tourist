About Rajeev Smritivan.

Built In the memory of the former Prime Minister Shri Rajiv Gandhi Ji, this park developed over a huge area of more than 14 acres is a must-visit place if you are visiting Raipur with your kids. It is sure to not make them realise the importance of preserving energy but also create awareness about the many benefits of solar energy. Located just 12kms away from the Raipur city, this place is open all days from 8 AM to 6:30 PM and is just the right place which guarantees you a good time with your family.
