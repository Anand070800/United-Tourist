About Peren.

Nagaland is one of the most scenic states in the country, but if there is a place in the state where you still find virgin and untouched forests it is the district of Peren. The inhabitants of this place have preserved the forests religiously which makes it one of biggest attractions in the state. It is bounded by Assam and Dimapur district on the west, district of Kohima in the east and the state of Manipur on the south. The town of Peren which is also the district headquarter is a paradise for the nature lovers.
