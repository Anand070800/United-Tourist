About Tawang.

One can take a 4-hour drive to Tezpur from the Guwahati airport and take a Tata Sumo or Mahindra Bolero from Tezpur to reach Tawang. Nearest Airport: Salonibari Airport is the nearest airport located in Tezpur, Assam. Lokapriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport in Guwahati is located 480 km from Tawang.
