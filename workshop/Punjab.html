About Punjab.

Located in the northwestern part of India, Punjab means the land of five rivers. Known for its cuisine, culture and history, Punjab is visited by tourists who want to enjoy its vibrant culture and food. The fertile land of farms and forts, Punjab has been aptly nicknamed the 'Smiling Soul of India' where you will always see smiles on the faces of the inhabitants who take pride in their religion and culture. Though the state does not have any significant adventure activity to offer, yet entertainment is guaranteed!
